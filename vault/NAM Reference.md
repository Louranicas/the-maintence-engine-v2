## NAM Compliance Target: 95%

The system must comply with NAM axioms across 5 rules:
- **R1:** Field Awareness (system observes its own state)
- **R2:** Dissent Capture (minority opinions are recorded)
- **R3:** Temporal Coherence (phase, not clock time)
- **R4:** Emergent Behaviour (system can surprise itself)
- **R5:** Non-Privileged Observer (no single point of authority)
